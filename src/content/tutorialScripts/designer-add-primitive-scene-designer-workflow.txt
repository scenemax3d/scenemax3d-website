designer.add_primitive, Scene Designer Workflow explains an assistant action for the scene designer.
Designer tools are needed because scene entities have names, transforms, materials, and resources that should be inspected before they are changed.
Use this when the assistant must add a primitive, update an entity, list entities, capture the canvas, overlay a reference, compare a view, or validate a document.
A concrete game task is adjusting an arena object in Dragon Gate: inspect the entity, change one material or position, capture the view, and compare the result.
The benefit is visual control over the scene instead of editing blindly.
