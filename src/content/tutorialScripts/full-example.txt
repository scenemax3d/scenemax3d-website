This full example uses effects the way a real combat scene uses them. The player gets a shield aura, and a beam effect fires toward the enemy.
[video: /assets/tutorials/effekseer-runtime/aim-effect-loop.mp4]
First, declare the player, enemy, shield effect, and beam effect. Attach the shield to the player and play it with loop, because a shield should stay active.
Then aim the beam at the enemy with look at, and play it from the player with a faster playback speed.
[video: /assets/tutorials/effekseer-runtime/show-hide-delete-loop.mp4]
At the end, hide and show the shield to demonstrate visibility control, then delete both effects to clean up.
