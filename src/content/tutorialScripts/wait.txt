wait pauses the current command flow for a specific amount of time.
The commands after wait do not run until the wait time is finished.
In this example, the warning appears first, then SceneMax waits 2 seconds, then the opened message appears.
Use wait for timing: delayed doors, delayed explosions, countdowns, stopping sounds, or letting an animation finish.
Wait is simple, but it is important because it controls the order and timing of events.
