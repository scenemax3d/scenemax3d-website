A range constraint gives a numeric variable allowed limits.
The range [0..20] means the value should stay from 0 to 20.
This helps protect gameplay values from becoming impossible, such as negative health or too much ammo.
In this example, health starts at 20 and the range describes the valid health area.
Use ranges for health, ammo, energy, timers, difficulty values, and anything that must stay inside safe bounds.
