A loop repeats the same commands.
Use a loop when the game should do something several times, such as spinning a reward, spawning enemies, or counting down.
The line do 4 times starts a block that repeats four times.
Every command between do and end do is part of the loop.
Loops keep scripts short and make repeated behavior easier to change later.
