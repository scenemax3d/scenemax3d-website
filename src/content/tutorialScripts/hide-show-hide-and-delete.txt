Hide turns the effect invisible. It does not remove the effect object from the scene.
The full beginner pattern is: declare the effect, play it, then call hide when it should disappear for a while.
Use hide when the same effect may come back later, such as a shield that turns off and on, or a portal marker that disappears during a cutscene. If you are completely finished with the effect, use delete instead.
