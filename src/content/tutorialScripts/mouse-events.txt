A mouse event runs code when the player clicks.
You can listen for a click anywhere, or you can attach the event to a specific object.
In this example, the command runs only when the player clicks on the Sinbad model.
That makes the click meaningful instead of reacting to every place on the screen.
Use mouse events for selecting units, clicking buttons in a 3D scene, opening chests, or inspecting objects.
