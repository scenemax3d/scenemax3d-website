Running at intervals repeats a procedure on a timer.
The phrase every 1.5 seconds tells SceneMax how often to run it.
In this example, tick can be used as a repeated spawn check.
Intervals are useful when a rule should happen over time but not every frame.
Use them for spawning enemies, updating status text, checking objectives, or creating timed effects.
