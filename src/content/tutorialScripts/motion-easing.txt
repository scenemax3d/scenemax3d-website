Motion Easing explains how motion becomes smoother and more intentional.
Use this when straight constant-speed motion feels too robotic. Easing gives motion a beginning, middle, and ending feel.
In a fighting game, this is what makes a punch, jump, dodge, replay path, or knockback feel responsive instead of random.
The sample begins with player.move forward 4 in 1.5 seconds ease in. In plain language, it moves something through the world using distance and time.
The key idea is to know what problem this feature solves before using it in a larger scene.
