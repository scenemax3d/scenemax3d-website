Use a fixed position when the effect belongs to a point in the world.
For example, a trap can flash at its location, a portal can glow in one place, and an explosion can appear where a projectile landed.
The numbers in pos are x, y, and z. In this sample, the ring effect appears at x three, y one, z seven. It does not follow a model; it plays exactly there.
