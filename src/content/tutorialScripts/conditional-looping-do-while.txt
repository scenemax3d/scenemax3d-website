We can control the amount of times a loop is running, using the **while** condition.
```
var counter = 5
d => dragon
[g]do
  d.turn left 30 in 1 second
  d.turn right 20 in 0.5 seconds
  counter = counter - 1
[g]while counter > 0
```
in this example, the dragon will turn left and then turn right 5 times, as long as the counter is greater than 0.

the counter variable is declared outside of the "do" block and is decreased by one inside the "do" block.
