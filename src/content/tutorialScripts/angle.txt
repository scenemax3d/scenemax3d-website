angle calculates the direction from one object toward another object.
Use it when something needs to face, aim, or compare direction.
In this example, aim_angle stores the angle from the player to the enemy.
The player can then turn toward that angle before attacking.
Angle is useful for aiming, enemy tracking, turret rotation, camera direction, and directional UI indicators.
