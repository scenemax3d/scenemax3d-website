Important Runtime Rule, Cinematic Camera explains how the camera supports gameplay or cinematic presentation.
Use this when the view has a job: following, framing, revealing, focusing, or adding emotion to the action.
In Dragon Gate style combat, the camera must keep both fighters readable, show distance clearly, and avoid losing the action during jumps or attacks.
The sample begins with intro_cam.play : target player1, duration 8. In plain language, it controls feedback the player can see or hear.
The key idea is framing: camera settings are gameplay settings because they decide what information the player can see.
