Attach to a joint when the effect must follow one body part, not the whole model.
Hands, heads, feet, and weapons often have named skeleton joints. Put the joint name in quotes after the model name.
Here, fighter1 is loaded with its RightHand joint, and hand_fx attaches to that joint. That is useful for a magic spell held in the hand or a sword trail connected to a weapon hand.
