Use this when the view has a job: following, framing, revealing, focusing, or adding emotion to the action.
In Dragon Gate style combat, the camera must keep both fighters readable, show distance clearly, and avoid losing the action during jumps or attacks.
The sample begins with cam_a=>cinematic.camera.close_orbit. In plain language, it creates or loads an object and gives it a name for later commands.
