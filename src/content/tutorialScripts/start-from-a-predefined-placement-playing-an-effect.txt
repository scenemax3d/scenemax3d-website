You can give an effect a starting placement when you declare it. This is useful when the asset needs a small offset or rotation before it looks correct.
In the sample, fire_fx is created with pos zero, one, zero, so it starts a little higher, and rotate zero, forty five, zero turns it.
Later, play pos enemy starts the prepared effect at the enemy. This keeps the play line simple while the setup line handles the adjustment.
