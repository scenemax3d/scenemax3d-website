Detach from parent breaks the attachment. After detaching, the effect no longer follows the model.
This is useful when an effect starts on a character but should stay behind, such as a dropped magical field or a smoke cloud.
In the sample, the aura starts attached to player. After one second, detach from parent makes it independent again.
