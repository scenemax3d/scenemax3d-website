Before you can play an Effekseer effect, give it a SceneMax name.
The left side, fire_fx, is the variable name you will use in later commands. The right side, effects dot effekseer dot A_Salamander1, points to the imported effect asset.
After this line, fire_fx behaves like a runtime object. You can play it, attach it, aim it, hide it, show it, or delete it.
