Delete, Show, Hide, And Delete explains how visuals make the scene clearer or more expressive.
Use this when the player needs clarity, mood, impact, or feedback from what appears on screen.
In an arena scene, visuals can show danger, make characters readable, and make a special attack feel important.
The sample begins with portal_fx.delete. In plain language, it shows the command or data shape used by this lesson.
The key idea is readability first. Effects and lights should support the action, not distract from it.
