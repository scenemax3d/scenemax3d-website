Upload Asks For Login Even Though Butler Exists, Troubleshooting explains a packaging or publishing step for a SceneMax game.
Publishing is needed because a project that works locally still has to become a build that players can access from the correct game page.
Use this when you need to package files, configure Butler, authenticate, choose the target page or channel, upload, or understand an upload error.
A concrete game task is releasing a Dragon Gate test build. The build folder, itch dot io account, game page, channel, and authentication must all match.
The benefit is a predictable release process instead of guessing why an upload failed.
